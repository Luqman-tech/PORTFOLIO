about

I'm a creator based in Nairobi, Kenya.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed creating experiences and products. From a young age I enjoyed making art pieces and would be satisfied with the joy expressed by others on their faces. As I have grown my interests have spread beyond art and into Technology and Project Management. Given that technology is all around us and determining our future, I saw it fit to take part in creating our collective future. With my interest in creating experiences, Project Management was an obvious choice in skills I required.
